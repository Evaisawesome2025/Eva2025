Our Story

Simple ingredients. Honest lemonade.

Glen's Lemons started with a simple idea: a glass of lemonade should taste like real lemons — nothing more, nothing less. No powders, no mystery mixes, no shortcuts.

Every batch is hand-squeezed fresh and stirred with just the right amount of sugar, then poured over ice for that crisp, puckery finish that makes a hot Sioux Falls afternoon feel a whole lot better.

Good to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

What's actually in it?

Three simple things: fresh-squeezed lemons, sugar, and water. Always hand-squeezed — never from a powder or mix.

Is it vegan and gluten-free?

Yes! Water, sugar, and lemons are naturally vegan and gluten-free, with no artificial flavors, colors, or preservatives.

Where can I find you?

At the Lake Lorraine Farmers Market in Sioux Falls, SD — every Thursday starting at 4:00 PM. Look for the lemon-yellow stand!

What sizes do you offer?

One size, done right: a full 24 oz cup of fresh squeezed lemonade for $5.

How can I pay?

We accept cash 💵 and cards 💳 — whatever's easiest for you.

Do you cater events?

Absolutely — birthdays, weddings, graduation parties, company picnics, and more. Check out our catering packages, or tap Inquire About Catering to send us the details.

How far ahead should I book catering?

The sooner the better, especially for busy summer weekends. Reach out as soon as you have a date and we'll lock it in.
