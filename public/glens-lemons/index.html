Our Story

Simple ingredients. Honest lemonade.

Glen's Lemons started with a simple idea: a glass of lemonade should taste like real lemons — nothing more, nothing less. No powders, no mystery mixes, no shortcuts.

Every batch is hand-squeezed fresh and stirred with just the right amount of sugar, then poured over ice for that crisp, puckery finish that makes a hot Sioux Falls afternoon feel a whole lot better.

— Glen 🍋
